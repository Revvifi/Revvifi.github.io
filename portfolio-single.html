Future Plans

#1. We aim to advocate for small business policy.

Many small businesses have been hit hard by the pandemic, and even thought the most devastating economic effects occurred over 2-3 years ago, many have failed to bounce back. With e-commerce traffic at an all time high, small businesses in the community, which play an important role in shaping both the identity and culture of the town, county, or state, need the proper resources and support to succeed.

Although just a group of high schoolers, Revvifi aims to work with leading politicans and other government officials across the United States to research and analyze current policy regarding small businesses. Our work in this field will hopefully include the development of possible policy proposals that will be presented to positively impact small businesses in the future.

#2. We aim to expand and have an impact across the US

As Revvifi continues to assist small business thrive in an ever-changing economy, one of the biggest parts of maximizing our impact is expanding across the United States. We aim to develop a branch/chapter based system to allow students to run their own versions of Revvifi and forge success in their local communities. Revvifi is on the verge of developing important resources to help you start up a charter and shape success around you!

Another thing to shape success nationwide is the possible development of small-business-oriented software to allow them to scale easier, gain more revenue.
